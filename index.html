Health Informatics and Telemedicine

The "Health Informatics and Telemedicine Laboratory" course at NIT Rourkela is a hands-on program focusing on healthcare technology. It covers mobile app development, IoT, cloud computing, data analytics, and healthcare data visualization, providing students with practical skills in health informatics and telemedicine.

IoT for healthcare monitoring

"IoT for Healthcare Monitoring is a transformative solution that melds state-of-the-art hardware and software. This innovative approach leverages the power of Arduino and Raspberry Pi, versatile microcontroller platforms, along with Bluetooth, Serial, and HTTPS protocols to enable seamless data exchange."

Design of an Application for mobile devices

Designing a mobile app with MIT App Inventor is a user-friendly and efficient process. Its visual, drag-and-drop interface simplifies app creation, making it accessible for beginners. It empowers creators to design, develop, and deploy Android apps with ease, promoting innovation .
